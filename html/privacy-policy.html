Privacy Policy

R2 TikTok Upload respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

When you connect your TikTok account, TikTok provides a temporary access token that allows this service to upload videos to your account. This token is stored securely in Cloudflare KV storage and is never shared with third parties.

You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
